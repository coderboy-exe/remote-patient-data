Introduction

Assume you move to a new location. new house, new environment, new people. You suddenly fall sick or have a relapse of an existing medical condition. Your health records are with the last health personnel you visited in your former location and your new health practitioner has no means of accessing them.


Many people today suffer from issues like this. They have problems accessing and sharing their personal health records to medical practitionaers when seeking medical advise and they dont have a proper documentation of medications used. We have the solution - a secure cloud based health records system.

Patient

The privacy and welfare of the patient is of utmost importance to us.

How To Use
  1. Sign up.
  2. Fill in the required information.
  3. Share your secure token with a trusted health professional.
  4. Health professional gains access to your personal health records.
Important

Note

The patient reserves the right to share his/her records with any medical personnel of their choice.

Warning
